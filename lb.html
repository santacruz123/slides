Here is the story and how Ripple network can help:

Today is Jan 2014

Alice must receive $1000 bonus at work in Jan 2015

But she needs money today to pay for new iPhone

She says "Lend me $900 today and I will return $1000 in Jan 2015"

In other words she is saying:



"I'm selling $1000 zero-coupon bond with expiration at Jan 2015 for $900 of Lendbitco.in"

$1000 = $1 * 1000:



"I'm selling 1000 zero-coupon bonds $1 with expiration at Jan 2015 for $0.9 Lendbitco.in each"

Detecting order


"I'm selling (ASK) 1000 (10000) zero-coupon bonds $1 (USD) with expiration at Jan 2015 (Jan 2015) for $0.9 (0.9 USD) Lendbitco.in each"

Order found



ASK 1000/USD/2015JAN/Alice @ 0.9 USD/Lendbitco.in

Transforming symbol into Ripple code



ASK 1000/USD(U)/2015(B)Jan(F)/Alice @ 0.9 USD/Lenditco.in


(Click down arrow to see rules)

The 1st of 3 letters - currency code:

B = BTC
X = XRP
U = USD
E = EUR
J = JPY
G = GBP

etc...

The 2nd of 3 - year code

A = 2014
B = 2015
C = 2016
D = 2017
E = 2018
F = 2019

etc...

The 3rd of 3 - delivery month

F = JAN N = JUL
G = FEB Q = AUG
H = MAR U = SEP
J = APR V = OCT
K = MAY X = NOV
M = JUN Z = DEC

like in futures codes

Done



ASK 1000/UBF/Alice @ 0.9 USD/Lendbitco.in

Putting address instead of name



ASK 1000/UBF/rQpCfA..... @ 0.9 USD/rZFRwH5......

"Lend me $900 today and I will return $1000 in Jan 2015"


becomes


ASK 1000/UBF/rQpCfA..... @ 0.9 USD/rZFRwH5......

If somebody wants to lend money to Alice


"I lend Alice $895 today and she will return $1000 in Jan 2015"


becomes


BUY 1000/UBF/rQpCfA..... @ 0.895 USD/rZFRwH5......

Somebody wants to lend only part of the amount


"I lend Alice $432 today and she will return $487 in Jan 2015"


becomes


BUY 487/UBF/rQpCfA..... @ 0.887 USD/rZFRwH5......
